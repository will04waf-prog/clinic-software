# First-customer walkthrough — the loop, on a phone

Follow this sitting next to a landscaper, both on phones. Goal: he signs
up, sends himself an estimate, and you (playing his customer) approve it —
so he *sees* a job appear. ~4 minutes. Everything is in Spanish.

**Two phones:** his (the owner) + yours (plays the customer who approves).

---

## 1. He signs up  (his phone)
- Open **tarhunna.net/es** → tap **Empezar** (or go straight to **tarhunna.net/signup**).
- **¿A qué se dedica su negocio?** → tap **Jardinería y paisajismo**.
- Fill: nombre del negocio, su nombre, **su celular** (required — this is where approvals land), correo, contraseña → **Empezar prueba gratis**.
- He lands on the **loop intro** (cliente → estimado → WhatsApp → cobra) → **Agregar mi primer cliente**.

*If the industry picker doesn't show Jardinería as tappable → hard-refresh once.*

## 2. He adds a client + builds an estimate  (his phone)
- From the dashboard tap **Estimados** → **Nuevo estimado**.
- **Cliente nuevo**: put a real name and **your** cell number (so the approval link comes to YOUR phone in the demo).
- **Título:** e.g. "Corte y limpieza de jardín".
- Add 1–2 **conceptos**: descripción + precio (e.g. Corte de césped $80, Limpieza $40). Watch the **Total** add up.
- **Guardar borrador**.

*Under 2 minutes is the target — if it's slower, that's the feedback to note.*

## 3. He sends it  (his phone)
- On the estimate, tap **Enviar por WhatsApp**.
- Today WhatsApp auto-send is pending Meta approval, so it may show the **link** instead of sending — that's fine: tap **copiar** and paste it into his own WhatsApp to you, OR just open the link on your phone. (Once Meta approves the templates, this sends automatically.)

## 4. You approve  (your phone = the customer)
- Open the link → a clean Spanish page: **"[su negocio] le envió un estimado"**, the line items, the total.
- Tap **Aprobar estimado** → **¡Estimado aprobado!**

## 5. He sees the job  (his phone)
- Back on his phone → **Agenda**. The approved estimate is now a **scheduled job**.
- That's the whole loop: *he made money move with his thumb.* That's the pitch.

---

## The close
Leave him on a live trial with a real client already in the system. Tell him:
- Every estimate he sends, the customer approves with one tap — no app, no login.
- Alerts and approvals come to his cell (WhatsApp coming; texts today).
- Card payments + "cobrar" are next (in build).

## If something goes wrong
- **Approve link says "no encontrado"** → the estimate wasn't sent yet, or the link was truncated when copied. Re-send from the estimate and copy the whole thing.
- **Nothing arrives on the customer phone** → expected until WhatsApp/A2P go live; use the copied link. This is by design (the link always works).
- **His dashboard looks like a med-spa (Layla, calls)** → his org's vertical isn't landscaping. Shouldn't happen from /signup; if it does, flag it — do not fix from the field.

## Hygiene
Demo signups are real trial orgs. Either keep the good one as his live account, or delete test orgs later by name. Never edit a real tenant from the field.
